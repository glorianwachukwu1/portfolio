About me

Hi, I’m Gloria Nwachukwu, and I’m actively seeking full-time job opportunities

I’m a data-driven, solution-oriented professional with a passion for designing intelligent systems that make organizations smarter, faster, and more resilient. My work sits at the intersection of technology, analytics, and business insight—I thrive on translating complex problems into actionable solutions, whether it’s optimizing operational processes, building scalable systems, or guiding cross-functional teams toward transformative outcomes.

I earned my Bachelor’s degree from Enugu State University and a Master of Science in Information Technology fromRensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), with a focus on Data Science and Analytics. My academic journey laid a strong foundation in machine learning, data analytics, coding, and the software development lifecycle. During this time, I also served as a Graduate Teaching Assistant under Prof. Brian Callahanfor web development and systems credentials, guiding over 48 students through lecture sessions, assignments, and hands-on projects. This experience sharpened my ability to communicate complex technical concepts clearly and effectively, and I was honored to receive RPI’s two most prestigious awards—the Founders Award of Excellence (Top 1% of students) and the Competitive Graduate Teaching Assistantship Award.

Professionally, I’ve had the privilege of contributing to high-impact organizations across public service, finance, and technology. I currently serve as a Business Systems Analyst at the New York State & Local Retirement System (NYSLRS), a $50B+ pension fund serving 800,000+ members. There, I strengthen the integrity of enterprise systems by validating high-stakes retirement financial data, conducting SQL-driven investigations, performing User Acceptance Testing (UAT) and System Integration Testing (SIT), and reducing post-deployment defects by 99%. Every day, I create clarity in environments where accuracy, trust, and accountability are non-negotiable.

Before that, I worked at NIRSAL Plc, a Central Bank of Nigeria, backed organization managing ₦500B+ in agricultural financing nationwide. I analyzed high-value credit s, supported project monitoring, and delivered financial education to over 1,000 agribusiness owners across five years. One of my proudest moments was representing NIRSAL Plc, at the National Youth Service Corps orientation camp in Imo State, addressing over 1,100 youth corps members; addressing over 1,100 youth corps members; a moment that reminded me of the power of communication and leadership in inspiring impact.

Alongside professional experience, I’ve applied my technical expertise to diverse research projects, ranging from heart disease prediction and solar-energy risk modeling to aircraft-loading systems for United Airlines, usability testing for e-commerce platforms, and competitive intelligence for tech startups. I’m proficient in Python, SQL, R, React, HTML/CSS, cloud tools, and analytics platforms, and I leverage tools like Visio, Figma and Jira to bridge business and technology seamlessly.

Outside of work, I am a STEM advocate, writer, motivational quote collector, educator, and community leader. I thrive on lifelong learning and aim to inspire those around me, not only through results but also through mentorship, collaboration, and example. My global experiences and multidisciplinary background shape my perspective, and I bring a balance of discipline, curiosity, and creativity to every team I join.

In short, I’m a problem solver, an innovator, and a lifelong learner, committed to making meaningful impact wherever I go. I combine technical expertise, business insight, and human-centered thinking to drive results—and I’m ready to bring that energy to the next challenge.
